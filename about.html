Native Land
Music Festival 2022

NATIVELAND is our annual music festival which brings the world of the NATIVE to life. The all-day event draws thousands of young West Africans to the iconic Muri Okunola Park, with a diverse line up for the music festival to inspiring talks from some of our communities most influential thought leaders. NATIVELAND brings together the best of the pop culture, music, art, food, style, sports, and education, ensuring that we’re shaping and celebrating the culture’s future.
